Waffle Recipe

Description

An easy go to waffle recipe you can make at home

This dish is filled with nutrients and protein.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Milk
  • 1 Cup Protein Waffle Mix
  • Berries
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine the waffle mix and the milk
  2. Whisk the ingredients gently, do not over whisk
  3. Fold in the berries and chocolate chips
  4. Add a bit of boil to your waffle maker, and add the waffle mix to the waffle maker
  5. Let cook for 5 minutes
