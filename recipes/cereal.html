Waffle Recipe

Description

An easy go to cereal recipe you can make at home in minutes.

This dish is filled with nutrients and vitamins.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 1 Cup your favorite cereal
  • Berries
  • Almonds

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine your cereal and milk
  2. Add a handful of berries and almond, enjoy!
