Steamed Egg Recipe

Description

When the waiter asks how you'd like your eggs, steamed is probably not the first thing that pops into your head. Similar to fried egg, scrammbled egg, or sunny side up egg, the steamed egg is just another variation of a breakfast egg.

Steamed egg is a popular chinese breakfast dish. The texture is silky like silken tofu and is typically served with soy sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • Water
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Whisk the egg in a small bowl
  2. Add 1/4 cup of warm water
  3. In a separate pot, add enough water so that the water comes halfway up the bowl
  4. Bring the water to a boil
  5. Place the bowl with the egg into the pot and cover with a lid
  6. Let steam for 5 minutes
  7. After 5 minutes, remove and add soy sauce
